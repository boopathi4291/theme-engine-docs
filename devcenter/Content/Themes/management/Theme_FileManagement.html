Theme Management

As you create your responsive themes, you can save that content to GitHub. Volusion will provide a Marketplace service for offering your themes to all merchants and designers. The repository is also accessed when uploading the theme for your Admin designs.

Currently, this information is In Progress. The information will provide details and best practices for managing the theme files, including all HTML, CSS, and images.
